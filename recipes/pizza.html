Ooey Gooey Cheesy Pizza

For this recipe we are first going to need a few things

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Fresh Pizza Sauce
  • Fresh Mozzarella Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Red Pepper Flakes (if you like it spicy)

Once you have all of your ingredients lined up and ready to go then you are ready to begin.

  1. Stretch your pizza dough to the size desired
  2. Use you utensil of choice to spread your sauce on the pizza dough
  3. Grate the mozzarella cheese over the sauce. (The amount is completely up to you)
  4. Grate some of the parmesan cheese but make sure that you leave some for when its done
  5. Sprinkle some of the red pepper flakes on

Now that you have your pizza assembled, all that's left to do is bake it! Heat your oven up to 450 degrees and cook it for 10-15 minutes. After it's done. Enjoy!!!
